What's GIS DAY?

GIS Day is an international forum for geographic information system (GIS) users to share their knowledge and applications of GIS technology. GIS day provides an opportunity for students of all ages, professionals, businesses and the public to discuss the impact GIS has on our daily lives.

2022 Competition

Monday, November 14

Each year, ATX GIS Day hosts a geospatial poster exhibition and competition.
This event is judged by a panel of audience members and experts in the field.
We invite all including professionals and students from colleges and universities in Texas to submit their
ongoing or completed geospatial work. Participants will have the opportunity to win $ prizes.
